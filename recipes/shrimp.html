Homemade Teriyaki Shrimp

Home

Description

This is a teriyaki shrimp dish bursting with sweet and umami flavors!

It works great a a lunch or a main dish if you add sides.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound frozen raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup coconut aminos
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons water
  • 2 cups steamed white rice
  • 1 tablespoon sliced green onions(Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds(Optional)
